Call Centre

We offer comprehensive call center services that include inbound customer care and loyalty programs. Our team recognizes the significance of delivering exceptional customer support, and our well-trained professionals are adapted at addressing a wide range of customer requirements. With a proven history of serving esteemed companies in the Loyalty industry, our aim is to go above and beyond expectations, fostering enduring relationships along the way.

Corporate Training

We offer specialized training programs in customer care and retail. Our training modules are designed to provide individuals with the necessary skills and knowledge to succeed in a competitive business environment. We have been chosen by notable clients like Mr Price Foundation to train their employees, showing that our programs are effective.

Learnership Training

As an approved training provider by SETA, we offer learnership programs that focus on skills development and youth entrepreneurial development. Our learnership options cover a range of fields, including contact centre operations, new venture creation (NVC), and administration. Red Alert and Toyota are among the esteemed organizations that have chosen us to train their learners and foster entrepreneurial growth.
